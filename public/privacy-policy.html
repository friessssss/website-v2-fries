RL Tracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 3, 2024

Overview

RL Tracker is a personal project that helps track Rocket League goals scored while listening to music on Spotify. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information through Spotify's API:

  • Currently playing track information: Song name, artist, album, and playback status
  • Playlist information: Playlist name and details when music is played from a playlist
  • Goal tracking data: Player names, timestamps, and song information when goals are scored

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display your currently playing Spotify track
  • Track and store goal data with associated song information
  • Generate analytics about goals scored during different songs
  • Provide a seamless gaming and music tracking experience

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using MongoDB Atlas. We do not share your personal information with third parties.

Spotify Integration

This app integrates with Spotify's API to access your music playback information. We only access the minimum required permissions:

  • Read your currently playing track
  • Read your playback state
  • Read playlist information

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your Spotify account at any time
  • Request deletion of your goal tracking data
  • Access the data we have stored about you

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the website owner's contact information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes.
